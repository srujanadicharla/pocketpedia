Common Causes of Bronchitis


  • Smoking
  • Exposure to high levels of second-hand smoke
  • Working in a stuffy atmosphere
  • Use of drugs
  • Weather changes, such as too much pollution, fog or dusty air
  • High doze of medicines
  • Improper diet
  • Unrest
  • Stress
  • Genetics and heredity reasons
  • Improper breathing technique
  • Absence of fresh oxygen
  • Infections
  • Complication of cold or cough
  • Allergy

Chapped Lips

Common Causes of Chapped Lips


  • Deficiency of vitamins, iron or essential fatty acids.
  • Allergic reaction to some chemicals.
  • Dehydration
  • Excessive licking of lips
  • Exposure to sun radiations
  • Smoking
  • Certain medications
  • Change in climatic conditions

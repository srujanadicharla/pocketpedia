Dehydration

Common Causes of Dehydration


  • Apart from inadequate intake of water, Dehydration can be caused by conditions like repeated episodes of Diarrhea, Vomiting, Excessive Sweating, Fever and other similar problems
  • Moreover, frequent urination due to Diabetes, excessive alcohol consumption or prolonged intake of medicines like diuretics, antipsychotics, blood pressure medications etc may also contribute in causing Dehydration. Burn victims are also susceptible to this problem.

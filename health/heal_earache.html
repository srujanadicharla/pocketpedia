Earache

Common Causes of Earache


  • Otitis externa
  • Otitis media
  • Ear infection
  • Ear disease
  • Nose, mouth, or throat infection
  • Injury to jaw bones
  • Arthritis of the jaw
  • An insect in the ear
  • Fungus growth in the ear
  • Wax buildup in the ear
  • Cold
  • Sinus infection
  • Poor sleeping
  • Blockage in the ear tube
  • Damage to the eardrum
